## Language Guidelines

### Do Use:
- "users" instead of "VUs" or "virtual users"
- "build up" instead of "ramp up"
- "wind down" instead of "ramp down"
- "how long" instead of "duration"
- "what to test" instead of "target endpoint"
- "test script" instead of "k6 script"

### Don't Use:
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Abbreviations (VU, API, HTTP) without context
- Assumptions about user knowledge
- Vague terms like "configure" without context

### Always Include:
- Clear "what will happen" previews
- "Why" explanations for locked sections
- Help links that are accessible but not intrusive
- Visual feedback for all actions
